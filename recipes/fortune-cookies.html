Fortune Cookies

  • Ingredients:
  • 2 large egg whites
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon almond extract
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 6 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 6 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons water
  • Instructions:
  • Preheat oven to 300°F (150°C).
  • Write fortunes on strips of paper about 4 inches long and 1/2 inch wide.
  • Mix the egg whites and vanilla and almond extracts until foamy but not stiff.
  • Sift the flour, cornstarch, salt and sugar and blend into the egg white mixture.
  • Add the water and vegetable oil and stir until smooth.
  • Place teaspoonfuls of the batter onto a greased cookie sheet, spacing them at least 3 inches apart.
  • Bake for 14 to 15 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  • Quickly remove the cookies from the baking sheet and place a fortune in the center of each cookie.
  • Fold the cookie in half, then fold the ends together to form the fortune cookie shape.
  • Place the cookies into the cups of a muffin tin to hold their shape until they cool.
