Focaccia Bread

Ingredients

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp rosemary

Instructions

  1. Dissolve yeast in warm water and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Mix in olive oil and salt.
  3. Add flour and knead until smooth.
  4. Let the dough rise for 1 hour.
  5. Press dough into a baking sheet, dimple with fingers, and sprinkle with rosemary.
  6. Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 20 minutes.
