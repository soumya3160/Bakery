Thumbprint Cookies

  • Ingredients:
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup any flavor fruit jam
  • Instructions:
  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  • Cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  • Mix in flour and vanilla until well blended.
  • Shape dough into 1 inch balls and place on ungreased baking sheets.
  • Make an indentation in the center of each ball using your thumb or a spoon.
  • Fill each indentation with jam.
  • Bake for 14 to 18 minutes in the preheated oven, or until lightly browned.
