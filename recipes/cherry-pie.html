Cherry Pie

Cherry Pie

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh cherries
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1 package pie crust

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix cherries, sugar, cornstarch, and almond extract.
  3. Place bottom crust in pie plate, add filling, and cover with top crust.
  4. Bake for 45-50 minutes.
