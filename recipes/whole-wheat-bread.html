Whole Wheat Bread

Ingredients

  • 3 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 1/4 cups warm water
  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Dissolve yeast in warm water and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Mix in honey, olive oil, and salt.
  3. Add flour and knead until smooth.
  4. Let the dough rise for 1 hour.
  5. Shape dough into a loaf and let it rise for 30 minutes.
  6. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 30 minutes.
