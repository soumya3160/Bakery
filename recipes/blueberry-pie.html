Blueberry Pie

Ingredients

  • 4 cups fresh blueberries
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 package pie crust

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix blueberries, sugar, cornstarch, and lemon juice in a large bowl.
  3. Place the bottom pie crust in a pie plate and add the blueberry filling.
  4. Cover with the top crust, seal the edges, and cut slits in the top to allow steam to escape.
  5. Bake for 50-55 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
  6. Let cool before serving.
