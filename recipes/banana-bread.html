Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Mash bananas in a bowl and mix with melted butter.
  2. Mix in sugar, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add flour and stir until combined.
  4. Pour batter into a greased loaf pan.
  5. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 1 hour.
